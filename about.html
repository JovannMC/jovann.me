Who?

Hey! I'm Jovann, aka JovannMC online. I'm a 14 year old from the Philippines who grew up with and loves technology and the internet.

I love hanging out with friends and doing stuff together, playing games, making people happy, content creation, and getting programming work done.

I have some experience with Java for SpigotMC, C# for Unity, and HTML/CSS/JS for web development. I also have some experience with creating thumbnails and video editing, and currently self-host numerous services on my alternate domain, femboyfurry.net.
